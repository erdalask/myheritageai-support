‚ùì Frequently Asked Questions

How does MyHeritage AI work?

MyHeritage AI uses advanced artificial intelligence to analyze facial features in your photos and provide an entertaining heritage estimate. Simply upload a clear, front-facing photo and let our AI do the rest!

How do I purchase analysis credits?

Tap the credits button (ü™ô) in the app to open the purchase menu. Choose from our available packages: 1 Analysis ($0.49), 5 Analyses ($2.49), or 10 Analyses ($3.99). Payment is processed securely through the App Store.

Can I restore my purchases?

Yes! If you've reinstalled the app or switched devices, tap the credits button and select "Restore Purchases" to recover any previously purchased credits.

Is my photo data safe?

Your privacy is important to us. Photos are analyzed securely and are not stored permanently on our servers. We do not share your photos or personal data with third parties.

Why did my analysis fail?

Analysis may fail if the photo doesn't have a clear, front-facing face, has poor lighting, or the image file is corrupted. Try uploading a different photo with good lighting and a clear view of your face.

How accurate are the results?

MyHeritage AI is designed purely for entertainment. The results are AI-generated estimates based on facial features and should not be considered scientifically accurate. For real ancestry information, we recommend professional DNA testing services.

How can I request a refund?

All purchases are processed through Apple's App Store. To request a refund, please visit reportaproblem.apple.com or contact Apple Support directly.
